About Crucible XR

Experience the future of fitness with Crucible XR, a sci-fi themed extended reality app for the Meta Horizon OS. Each crucible is a series of fitness challenges designed to get your heartrate up and exercise your strength, agility, hand-eye coordination and intestinal fortitude in the face of a robot invasion.

Real Life Movement

Game levels include:

  • AR Robot boxing
  • AR Drone swatting
  • Final VR boss battle
Social Features
  • Robotic coaches to motivate and teach
  • Leaderboards
  • Health and Fitness analytics
  • Co-op and Competitive modes to play with friends.

Please give the prototype a try to experience a futuristic fitness adventure.

Name: Andrew Towl

Position: CEO and Chief Creative Officer

Skills: Creative Vision, Unity development, 2D and 3D art, Saber Juggling

Bio: Andrew Towl is a juggler turned game developer, Extended Reality expert, musician and artist. He has a Bachelor of fine arts degree and a Masters in Art and Technology from the University of Oklahoma with a focus on Augmented Reality, performance, entertainment and interactivity. With a background in visual communication and performance, he is constantly looking to push the bounds of Mixed Reality. Drawing on 10 years as a freelance illustrator he moved into game development in early 2016 and Augmented and Virtual Reality soon after. He is also a graduate with high honors from XRBootcamp.

With various teams he has won Best in Gaming and Storytelling at AT&T's Entertainment App Hackathon 2018, Best over all AT&T's Mixed Reality Hackathon 2019, 2nd Place overall at Magic Leaps Mixed Reality Competition 2018, and took Honorable Mention at Niantic's 2019 Beyond Reality Competition. Most recently he and his team took Best in WebXR using 8thwall's VPS(visual positioning system) at Niantic's VPS World Tour LA 2022.
